Follow the arrow to reach your destination.

To begin this challenge, you must ask the MOB for the Challenge 7 password and input it below. In this challenge, you are required to navigate between three locations across the NHAS. When you are within 10 metres of the location, a "Check in" button will appear, allowing you to register that checkpoint as captured. Once you have visited all three, you will be given a secret code that must be presented to the MOB to earn your points.

This challenge depends on your mobile device's inbuilt GPS and Compass, as such, technical glitches can occur. We suggest that your whole team has their phones out on this page and conducts the exercise together to eliminate any anomalous devices with malfunctioning compasses. You should also avoid large metal structures or each other's devices to prevent crosstalk. Good luck.

You must grant access to your location and compass to conduct this serial. Your location is not shared and will not be tracked once this page is closed.
